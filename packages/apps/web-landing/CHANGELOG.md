## 1.4.0

### Zero-Touch Operations

Added deployment automation, SLO monitoring, cost tracking, and anomaly detection capabilities.

### New Features

- Progressive delivery with canary and blue-green strategies
- Real-time SLO monitoring with burn-rate alerts
- Per-operation cost tracking and budget management
- Automated anomaly detection with root-cause analysis
- Operational runbooks for deployment safety

### New Libraries

- `@contractspec/lib.progressive-delivery` – strategies, controllers, traffic shifting, rollback manager
- `@contractspec/lib.slo` – definitions, tracker, burn-rate monitor, incident integration
- `@contractspec/lib.cost-tracking` – sample attribution, budgets, optimization recommender
- `@contractspec/lib.observability` – anomaly toolkit (baseline calculator, detector, root-cause analyzer, alert manager)
