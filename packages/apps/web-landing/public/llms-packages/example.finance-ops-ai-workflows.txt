# @contractspec/example.finance-ops-ai-workflows

Safe finance operations AI workflow example with mission intake triage, cash prioritization, procedure drafting, reporting narrative, human review, and adoption ROI logging.

## What This Demonstrates

- Turning finance ops requests into explicit ContractSpec operations instead of prompt-only workflows.
- Deterministic mission intake triage for DAF / finance transformation scoping.
- Fixed-date cash aging prioritization using synthetic receivables and rule-based actions.
- Procedure draft and reporting narrative helpers that keep management validation explicit.
- AI adoption ROI logging that measures use cases, time saved, data risk, quality, and policy needs.

## Interactive Preview

- Home screen with scenario loader and mission-control metrics.
- Mission intake screen with risks, documents, questions, and 30/60/90 plan.
- Cash aging screen with exposure totals, top priorities, and action pack.
- Procedure screen with roles, steps, controls, KPIs, and open questions.
- Reporting screen with deterministic KPI variance highlights and follow-ups.
- Adoption ROI screen with workflow-level usage logs and recommendations.

The package uses synthetic fixtures and deterministic handlers only. Outputs are review drafts and human review is required before any client-facing or operational use.

## Running Locally

From `packages/examples/finance-ops-ai-workflows`:
- `bun run build`
- `bun run typecheck`
- `bun run test`
- `bun run smoke`
- `bun run preflight`

## Demo Flow

1. Open `/templates` and click Preview on `Finance Ops AI Workflows`.
2. Switch between the PME recovery and reporting reset fixture scenarios.
3. Use Mission intake and Cash aging to demonstrate scoping and cash action workflows.
4. Use Procedure and Reporting to demonstrate operational artifacts beyond prompts.
5. Use Adoption ROI and the review drawer to close on measurable workflow adoption.
